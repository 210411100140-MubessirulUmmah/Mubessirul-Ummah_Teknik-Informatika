I saw some Burma Shave style sigma signs on the side of the road today:

Passing cars, When you can't see, may get you, A glimpse, Of eternity.

I definitely won't be passing any cars.

That's where the

element comes in. Unlike the element, which is meant for short quotes that are part of an existing paragraph, the
element is meant for longer quotes that need to be displayed on their own.

Passing cars,
When you can't see,
may get you,
A glimpse,
Of eternity.
